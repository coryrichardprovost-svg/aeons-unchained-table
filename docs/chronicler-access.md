# Grant Chronicler Access

New accounts sign up as Trailblazers by default.

Create the Chronicler account through the app with a real email.

1. Go to `/auth`.
2. Choose Sign Up.
3. Username: `The Chronicler`
4. Email: your real email for the Chronicler account.
5. Password: your chosen password.

The app gives the reserved username `The Chronicler` the `chronicler` role. Use a different email for a separate Trailblazer test account.
